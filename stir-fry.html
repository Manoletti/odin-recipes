Asian Stir Fry

Ingredience

  • Neutral vegetable oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Beef strips
  • Broccoli
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sichuan Peppers

Steps

  1. Warm up the Wok
  2. When it's piping hot, add some neutral vegetable oil
  3. Drop your beef strips and give them a quick stir for 2 mins
  4. Take the beef out and reserve the oil
  5. Repeat step 3 for the broccoli sprouts
  6. When they are bright green at almost done, add in your chopped peppers
  7. Add in your beef, and the other ingredients
  8. If needed, dissolve cornstarch in cold water and add it at the end
  9. Serve!
