Patacón

Ingredienceh3>
  • Green plantain
  • Pinch of salt
  • A deep pot filled with oil

Steps

  1. Warm up the oil while you slice the plantain in 2 in slices
  2. Give a first fry to the plantain knobs until they are golden and cooked inside
  3. Put pressure on them until flat with a flat botommed item
  4. Give it a last fry
  5. Serve when golden and crispy and sprinkle salt on them while hot
