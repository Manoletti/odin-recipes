Smoothie

Ingredience

  • Greek Yogurt (Double Protein)
  • Frozen Blueberries
  • Frozen strawberries
  • Half a banana
  • A pinch of cinnamon
  • A tiny pinch of salt
  • A tsp of honey

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend them
  3. Add ice if unsatisfied with consistency
  4. Serve on your desired plate and garnish with fresh fruits!
